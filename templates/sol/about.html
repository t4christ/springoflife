About us

Who we are

Vision

To take the Gospel of Christ to places where the light of God's knowledge is absent, where the Gospel has not been heard and the power of God not known. Romans15:16-21

Mission

We exist to honour God. We do so by planting Christ-centered, Spirit-empowered, socially responsible churches and schools in every nation.

Statement of Faith

We believe ...

  • i. That the Bible is inspired, authoritative and infallible in its teachings, facts and events. 2 Timothy 3:16.
  • ii. That God is a triune being, He is One in a very different sense from the sense in which He is three. He is one in His Spiritual essence ( Deut.6:4) and plural in the distinctiveness of His personalities ( Genesis 1: 26; 11 :7, Isaiah 6 : 8 ). The New Testament reveals three personalities within the God head; the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. Act 17:29, Mathew 28:19, 2Corithians 13:14.
  • iii. That Jesus Christ is deity born of a virgin, Mary ( Isaiah 7 :14; Matthew 1 :23, John 1: 1 -3,14).
  • iv. That the dead, burial, and ressurection of Jesus Christ is the only means of Grace for the saving of the soul of man. 1 Corinthians 15 : 1 - 4.
  • v. In believers baptism by immersion (for places without persecution and where there is the presence of a body of water). Matthew 28 : 18 - 20.
  • vi. In believers' transformation through the application of God's Word and the sanctifying ministry of the Holy Spirit and their collaborative obedience. Acts 26 :18; 2 Thessalonians 2:13; 1 Peter 1:2; 1 Thessalonians 4 : 3 - 4.
  • vii. In the present supernatural ministry of the Holy Spirit who bestows spiritual gifts and fruits on believers. 1 Corinthians 12, Galatians 5 : 22 - 23.
  • viii. In the physical ressurection of saved believers at the second coming of Jesus Christ. 1 Corinthians 15 : 52, 1 Thessalonians 4 : 16.
  • ix. In the final judgement of the unsaved. Revelations 20 : 11 - 15.
  • x. Eternal rewards of the Saints. Daniel 12 : 1 - 3, 2 Corinthians 5 : 10, Luke 19 : 17, Matthew 25: 15 - 21.

Core Values

In SOL our core values articulate for us the good deposit that is the core of our identity and how we do ministry. Whether we are building a discipleship group, planting a new church, teaching in our educational schools etc, our core values help describe who we are and how we build.

Lordship

Because Jesus is “King of kings and Lord of lords,” we believe that whole-hearted submission to God’s will and his Word is the starting point of faith and the foundation of all spiritual growth. Colossians 2 : 6.

Evangelism

Because God’s heart is to reach the lost, we are passionate about preaching the gospel and doing ministry in a way that engages people outside of the Christian faith. We seek to build churches primarily through evangelizing areas where no churches previously exist, and in areas that can act as catalyst for the fulfilment of the Great Commission ( even in such areas, we seek conversion rather than transfer growth, birth rather adoption). John 3 :16; Luke 19:10.

Discipleship

Because we’re called to make disciples, our primary focus is establishing biblical foundations, equipping believers to minister, and empowering disciples to make disciples. Matthew 28 : 19 - 20.

Cross-cultural

Cross-cultural means we take into congnisance, the language, ethnos and culture of a people and place. We recognise that to reach the unreached and to touch the untouched we will need to be cross-cultural in our evangelism and discipleship. The vision of God's kingdom is multi-cultural ( Revelation 7:9), SOL seeks to do its part towards the realization.

Leadership

Because we’re called to establish churches and start schools( in areas where such do not exist), we are committed to a culture of empowering leadership. We’re intentionally multi-generational, and we deliberately create opportunities and platforms to develop the next generation of leaders. 2 Timothy 2 : 2.

Socially Responsible

Socially Responsible Our love for Jesus and our compassion for people compel us to be socially responsible, which includes addressing social injustice, developing people to a point of self-reliance, and providing emergency relief. We always desire spiritual transformation through the gospel. The following scriptures form our grid for social responsibility: Micah 6 : 8. Matthew 25 :35,40. Matthew 5:13,14,15. Ephesians 2 :10

Family

Because the family is the foundation and validation of ministry, we refuse to sacrifice our marriages and our children on the altars of temporal success. And because we believe God has called us to be a spiritual family, we embrace community, reject the idea of disposable relationships, and choose to walk in love, respect, and unity. Psalm 127 : 1 - 3.

Board of Trustees

Mrs. Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma

Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma was born on the 29th of September 1956 to Bishop J.U.Itegboje who devoted his life to missionary works in Auchi and around Estako and Akoko-Edo local government areas of Edo State. In those years when Auchi was still predominantly Moslem, Bishop Itegboje fearlessly preached the gospel, confronting principalities and powers as he moved around the town in prayers late in the night, returning home in the early hours of the morning. The same deep and fearless missionary grace that was in Bishop J. U. Itegboje of blessed memory now rests firmly on her daughter. God in His rich mercies have ensured a change of baton from father to daughter. Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma attended Federal Government college Ibillo and went further to obtain her diploma certificate in teaching at Imaguero teacher's college Benin City. Shortly after her studies, she got married to Mr Brandon Nwakanma who spent his career as a diplomat serving in various countries including the United States of America, Somalia, Greece, Equatorial Guinea etc. Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma received the call to full time ministry in an encounter with the Lord. In this vision the Lord asked her to give up her lucrative business and to do His business. She subsequently obeyed the Lord, traveling the North and South of Nigeria in missionary forays. Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma was before her encounter with the Lord a business woman and was an importer of goods to Nigeria. She was the founder of Rose Maggie Nigeria Limited. After heeding the call of God, she joined Springs of Life Evangelistic Ministry(SOL) in the year 2001. She has served meritoriously and selflessly in SOL as an evangelist, teacher and a strong advocate for missions involvement and support. It is an honour and a privilege to have Mrs Margaret Olufemi Nwakanma serve as Chairman of the board of trustees of the Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL).

Henry Oise

Henry Oise is the lead missioner of the Springs of life global evangelistic Ministry(SOL). The ministry honours God by planting churches in places where no church previously existed as well as starting free-tuition schools in places where no school exists. Henry Oise has two bachelors degrees in Geography(Bsc.ed) from Delta State University, Abraka and in Missiology from the West Africa Christian University (WACU) Sango-Ota. He is an Alumni of the Christian leadership training Institute( CLTS) Ibadan. Henry Oise has authored four books and he is in his twentieth year in his missionary endeavours. The ministry he leads has four mission fields : OWUDEKUDU mission field ( which compromises of 9 villages). AKOGUN mission field( which compromises of six villages). ISHOKO mission field ( which compromises of 10 villages) and ONIDUNDUN mission field( which compromises of eleven villages). The mission runs two free-tuition schools in Owudekudu and Ishoko villages of Ogun state. Books, uniforms and writing materials are provided freely for these kids. The primary school at Owudekudu has 138 children while the primary school at Ishoko ( which is barely six months old) has 66 children. Because of the limited resources SOL has a ceiling on the number of kids they can admit by session. The schools go by the name : New Life Academy, and it recently was approved by the Ogun state ministry of education. The Churches SOL plants in the villages go by the name : New Life Bible Church. The Focus of SOL's evangelism and discipleship efforts so far is amongst the Yorubas and the Adja ethnic groups. The Adja people are from Benin republic and majority of them are farmers who in recent times have settled in large numbers in Ogun state .

Dr.Aigbe Gregory Ohihoin

Dr.Aigbe Gregory Ohihoin. MBBS(Ben),MSc (Lag),FMCOG(Nig),FWACS(WA),MCIArb(UK),DMAS(Del),PGDCR(Liverpool) Dr Aigbe Gregory Ohihoin is a Consultant Obstetrician Gynaecologist and a Chief Research Fellow at the Nigerian Institute of Medical Research. His undergraduate and post-graduate trainings are spread over three continents of the world. His basic medical degree was obtained from the University of Benin, Benin City Nigeria and he obtained a Masters degree in Physiology from the University of Lagos. He is a Fellow of the West African College of Surgeons and the National Post-graduate Medical College, Faculty of Obstetrics and Gynaecology. He holds a Fellowship and Diploma in the field of Minimal Access Surgery from Asia’s Premiere Institute of Laparoscopy, the Laparoscopy Hospital in Delhi India. He further obtained a diploma in Clinical research from the University of Liverpool. He is an alumnus of the Japan International Cooperation agency (JICA). Dr. Ohihoin has been admitted into the membership of several local and international medical associations including the Nigerian Medical Association, Society of gynaecology and Obstetrics of Nigeria (SOGON), Association for Fertility and Reproductive health (AFRH) and the World Association of Laparoscopic Surgeons. He is the founder of Nigeria’s first online maternity (www.pregnancynigeria.com). He has authored six books in his specialty –Obstetrics and Gynaecology and contributed chapters to other authors along with over forty publications in peer review journals and varied academic resources. His current research interest at the Nigerian Institute of Medical Research is on fertility care and regulation in patients who are infected with HIV infection. Apart from his medical training, Dr.Ohihoin is a trained Arbitrator and Mediator with a bias towards resolving disputes in the Health sector. He was enrolled as a member of the Chartered Institute of arbitrators of the United Kingdom, after becoming the first medical doctor to pass the accelerated membership exams. Shortly after, he was enrolled as a CEDR accredited mediator by exams also becoming the first medical doctor to have achieved this feat in the country. He has been a member of the LMDC Panel of Neutrals. His experience in mediation is largely, but not restricted to the health sector, where he has been involved in several high profile mediations. This has driven his research interest in introducing Alternative Dispute Resolution clauses to health sector settings. He founded the Medical Dispute and Conflict Resolution Limited- an organization committed to advocacy and training in the field of medical mediation and dispute resolution. Dr Gregory uses most of his vacation to serve the LORD as a medical missionary in SOL mission fields as well as those of other mission agencies and churches. He is married to his best friend, Esther and they are blessed with two children. We are honoured to have him serve as a trustee of the Springs of life Global evangelistic ministry (SOL).
