Who we are

Vision

Take the gospel of Christ to places where the light of God's is dim, where the gospel has not been heard and the power of God not known. Romans15:16-21

Mission

To honour God by planting Spirit empowered, Christ centered churches and schools in every nation.

Statement of Faith

We believe ...

  • i. That the Bible is inspired, authoritative and infallible in its teachings,facts and events
  • ii. That God is a triune being,He is one in a very different sense from the sense in which He is three. He is one of His Spiritual essence and three in the distinction between the three personalities within the God head. Act 17:29, Mathew 28:19, 2Corithians 13:14.
  • iii. That Jesus Christ is deity born of a virgin mary.
  • iv. That the dead burial and ressurection of Jesus Christ is the only means of Grace for the saving of the soul of man.
  • v. In believers baptism by immersion (for places without persecution and where their is the presence of a body of water).
  • vi. In believers' transformation through the application of God's word and the sanctifying ministry of the Holy Spirit.
  • vii. In the present supernatural ministry of the Holy Spirit who bestowed spiritual on believers.
  • viii. In the physical ressurection of saved believers at second coming of Jesus Christ and the final judgement of the unsaved.
