Educational Missions

Access to education changes lives now and for generations to come. This is a long-term suitable impact.

When communities are poverty stricken, in the short term it will be necessary to meet people's immediate needs in the form of food, shelter and clothes. SOL does all of these, but our goal is always long term-missions that transforms and restores. We believe one of the most strategic ways to bring about transformation of communities in the long term is through education. We establish tuition free schools where none exists.
